GAAP Logic is a very smart decision application that helps you interpret even the most complex accounting guidance. We believe that the application of accounting rules should not be the private domain of very expensive accounting firms and subject matter experts. Application of GAAP is usually a methodical decision process that lends itself to decision analysis. GAAP Logic is just that, a decision analysis application designed to get you to the right answer while saving you time and money.

We envisioned GAAP Logic as a crowd-sourced app, with user input into Help, Examples and other content as a critical feature. We also welcome feedback on functionality, bugs and anything else that occurs to you that would improve the user experience and make the app a world-class accounting ressearch tool. We have built in an Issues feature for commenting on anything and everything. We will monitor Issues and do our best to respond to all Issues raised with complete transparency. All Issues and related comments/resolutions are available for view and comment by all users. So have at it!
